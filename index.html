FAQ

How is this even possible?

Changing your star sign is a simple process, thanks to this one weird fact:

Star signs only have meaning in the pseudoscience of astrology, and any value that you attach to that. Once you realise this, it's easy understand that star signs are as malleable as any other part of human identity.

You can change your star sign with or without the help of this site.

Does this change my date of birth?

No, your date of birth remains the same. This only changes your star sign.

How does this work, though, really?

This site works by decoupling your star sign from your date of birth so that your star sign is no longer determined by the date of your birth, if it previously was.

It's a simple, painless process. It's free, and you can change your mind at any time. Just click the button, and start using your new star sign.

Can I change my star sign back?

Yes, you can change your star sign back to your original one at any time. Just select your original star sign from the list above.

The vast majority of people are very happy after changing their star sign; virtually nobody regret their decision.

How many times can I change my star sign?

You can change your star sign as many times as you want, whenever you want.

Why would I want to change my star sign?

People change their star sign for all sorts of reasons, including:

  • Choosing a star sign that better represents their personality or identity.
  • Improving their romantic compatibility with a partner of a normally low-compatibility star sign.
  • Trying on a new star sign to see if it suits them.
  • Avoiding an unlucky horoscope, or making the most of a lucky one belonging to a different star sign.
  • Method acting when performing a role whose character has a different star sign.

What if people don't accept my new star sign?

Unfortunately, some people hold the bigoted belief that star signs are fixed and immutable. These people are wrong.

You can show them the certificate of your new star sign, which is absolutely as valid as anybody else's. You're also welcome to point them at this FAQ. But - sadly -for some people, nothing will convince them.

This can be an warning sign that these people might hold other unpaletable beliefs, e.g. about sex, gender roles, marriage, sexuality, gender identity, race, etc. Probably you shouldn't be friends with such people in the first place.

What if my friend changes their star sign?

That's great! Congratulate your friend on their new star sign and be sure to refer to them properly in the future.

For many people, star signs don't come up in everyday conversation very often, so it can be easy to forget that your friend has a different star sign than you might have remembered. If you make a mistake, apologise and move on and endeavour to do better in future, just as you would if you incorrectly addressed your friend by a former name or using the wrong pronouns.

Can I change somebody else's star sign or give them a certificate as a gift?

It's not possible to change somebody else's star sign without their knowledge and consent.

However, you can give them a certificate of their new star sign as a gift. It's up to them whether or not they accept it and adopt their new star sign. Please be understanding if they don't: changing your star sign is a personal choice.

Can I change my star sign to something not on this list?

We understand that some people don't prescribe to the zodiac duodenary. Unfortuntately, this site can only help you with the classical Western twelve signs.

But if you'd like to change your star sign to e.g. Ophiuchus, to no specific sign, or to anything else, that's fine. Feel free to make your own certificate based on the ones available here, if you need one.
